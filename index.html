Hybrid Warrior Score

One number that summarizes how you are trending across the program. It combines:

  • Strength — sum of the bench, squat, and deadlift 1RM numbers in your profile.
  • Run — reward for a faster 4-mile pace (converted toward a 5K estimate).
  • Consistency — streak and how often you have logged in the last month.

It is a motivational snapshot, not a medical score. Use goals and logs for precise progress.
